Frequently Asked Questions

Is my document secure?

Yes, all documents are encrypted end-to-end and are secure from start to finish.

How fast is the delivery?

The delivery is instant, as soon as you hit send, the document reaches its destination.

Can I undo the send if I entered the wrong email?

Unfortunately, once a document is sent, it cannot be undone. Please double-check the email before submitting.

How can I contact customer support?

You can email us at senditSUPP0RT@gmail.com for assistance.

Can I send documents to multiple recipients at once?

Currently, we support sending to one recipient at a time. However, you can send multiple emails separately.

What file types can I send?

You can send PDFs, Word documents, images (JPG, PNG), and more.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size you can send is 4.5 MB.

Do you share my email or data with third parties?

No, we respect your privacy and do not share your data with any third parties.
